Visual Identity

From small businesses starting to develop their presence online, to individuals working within large organisations, it's important to maintain a solid personal brand online. Your brand and identity are key drivers of your company’s perception. Here at Creativo we mix creativity with our design talent to produce branding and identity that is both purposeful and unforgettable. Your company will catch the eye ads, event posters, and business cards that are attractive to your audience. From color schemes to logo designs and everything inbetween we have you covered.

Web Design

In today's technology driven world your website will be the first point and time a potential customer interacts with your business. Needless to say, it’s of utmost importance to make a great impression online just as your business does at its brick and mortar location. You need a website that grabs your potential customers attention, tells your story, and build trust through an online connection. Luckily for you this is what we do best. We develop websites that turn traffic into leads, leads into sales and customers into raving fans.

Mobile Design

As a fully responsive web design agency we know the importance of making sure your website provides an optimal viewing experience across a wide range of devices. So whether a customer views your site on a smartphone, tablet, or a 28” monitor you can rest rest assured they will have the best user experience possible. Furthermore, we integrate functionally responsive elements into your website like click-to-call functionality, making your customers just a tap away.

Development

Developing your online presence does not start and end at building a website or mobile application. We provide valuable insight and about social media, SEO, web analytics, and other up-and-coming technologies.We also take care of the standards compliancy of your web site, the content management system, the e-commerce system, the web and database development and the hosting, domain name registration, email and everything else in between.

The Creativo Process

When your business decides to create a new website or mobile app we can understand why it can turn into a frustrating, confusing, and daunting ordeal. There is an absurd amount of options and business to chose from, and each one will claim they are the best and better than the rest. But here at Creativo we do things a bit different than other web development firm.

Listing to your need

1

Before we get the ball rolling, we believe it’s critical to have an in person conversation to establish your needs, goals, and dreams. This first meeting will help us create a baseline to work from and build upon during the next stages. Furthermore, Because there is a possibly we may not the right fit for your business, and if thats the case we will make sure your business finds a more appropriate fit.

Project Discovery

2

We encounter many customers and competitors that at this stage are ready to just dive in headfirst and begin building. Unfortunately, it is not quite that easy. Before beginning we conduct market research to validate the proposed project. And by the end of this step we will create the perfect solution for your business that will not only provide what your customer is looking for but what they want.

Storming our Brains

3

Good projects come from good ideas. You are experts when it come to your business and customers and we are the experts when it comes to digital multimedia solutions. In this step we will build on our initial foundation with the use of visual aids such as wireframe and Photoshop mockups to give you the ability to visually see the flow and architecture of your new project. This will grant us the ability to work together and make modifications before the first building block is laid.
